NFC Sculpture Workshop
Build small clay sculptures with NFC tags embedded inside that trigger shortcuts on your phone — half art project, half hardware hack, at a DIY electronics bookstore with a no-one-turned-away-for-lack-of-funds door policy. Iffy Books keeps the barrier floor as low as it gets: $4.50 for the kit, pay what you can for instruction. If you've been wanting to start embedding tech into physical objects, this is the room to start in Philly.

⭐ Top 3 Picks
1
Speculative Futures Reading Group: 'The Ones Who Walk From Omelas' by Ursula Le Guin
Three linked stories — Le Guin's 'The Ones Who Walk From Omelas,' NK Jemisin's direct response 'The Ones Who Stay and Fight,' and Isabel J. Kim's newer riff — make this a living conversation across generations of speculative fiction writers about complicity and collective action. Literary theory meets leftist ethics meets actual fiction at a DIY bookstore with a donation jar. All three texts are available online; come prepared or come to listen.
Iffy Books
7:00 PM · donation to store requested

Lip Critic w/ Flatwounds + Bejalvin
